How It Works

01

Enable Camera

Grant webcam access when launching a game. The server processes frames securely, and your API keys never touch the client.

02

Stand Back

Move back 5-7 feet from your screen so the camera sees your upper body and legs. Make sure your play area is well lit.

03

Control with Gestures

Say "walk" or "go" to move forward, and "stop" to stand still. Lean left/right to slide, and pinch your fingers to shoot or reload verbally / physically.
