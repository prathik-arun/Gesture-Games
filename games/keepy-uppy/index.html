NEON KEEPY-UPPY

Bounce glowing helium balloons off your head and hands, or blow them up with mouth wind! No keyboards. No controllers.

How to Play
HEAD BOUNCE 🤕 - Move your head near balloons to bounce them back up.
HAND SHIELD 🖐️ - Wave your hands to slap balloons in the air.
MOUTH WIND 🌬️ - Open mouth wide to blow an upward gust of wind.
LIVES 💔 - Lose a heart if any balloon touches the laser floor. Spawns more balloons as score rises!
Fallback: Move cursor to position safety paddle. Spacebar/Click to blast wind. 'C' toggles background.
