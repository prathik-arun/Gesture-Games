How to Play (Gesture Guide)

✊

Fist: Hover / Move

Make a closed Fist (or relax your hand) to move the brush cursor around the board without drawing or erasing.

☝️

Point Finger: Action

Extend only your Index finger. When the Pen tool is selected, this draws lines. When the Eraser tool is selected, this erases lines.
